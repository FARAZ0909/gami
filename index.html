How this works right now: your browser's microphone listens (put the WhatsApp call on speaker), transcribes the Portuguese in real time, then sends each line off for translation and reply suggestions. It needs Chrome or Edge on desktop, or Chrome on Android — Safari and iOS don't support the speech recognition this prototype relies on. Nothing is recorded or stored; audio is processed live and discarded.
