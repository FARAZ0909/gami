Reserve a Table

The counter and tables fill quickly on Friday nights. Let us know when you're coming, or book instantly through our reservation partner.

Prefer an instant booking? Use carbonaraapp.com or call +351 933 679 725.

Good to know

01
Small room, big demand

The izakaya seats just a few dozen guests — reservations are strongly recommended Thursday to Saturday.

02
Hold time

Tables are held 15 minutes past the booked time before being released to walk-ins.

03
Broths take time

Our broths are simmered for hours, not minutes — kitchen tickets can run a little slower at peak times.
